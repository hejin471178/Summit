Summit Technology & Resources Inc. partners with pioneers that help guide infrastructural development toward a green and carbon-zero future. Among a cohort of advanced manufacturers, Summit is proud to present energy solutions crafted by CNBM (Chengdu) Optoelectronic Materials Co., Ltd.

ABOUT CNBM (CHENGDU) OPTOELECTRONIC MATERIALS CO., LTD.

As an advanced manufacturer, CNBM provides carbon neutral methods such as through CdTe Thin Film Solar Panels BIPV (Building-Integrated Photovoltaics). Marketed as CdTe Power Glass, CNBM's specialization manifests in a two-pronged approach: design integration into contemporary architecture; and the production efficiency of photovoltaic cells. Such solutions can blend into areas such as commercial malls, parking lots, and even office buildings as part-and-parcel with a modern design ethos. Its photovoltaic projects, meanwhile, show consistent capability of providing real world power generation above theoretical measurements.

PROJECT CASE

CNBM's solutions presently exist at places such as Yibin Yangtze River Park Huawei Direct Store and Wanhua Chemical Yantai Industrial Park Gymnasium, and are slated to be implemented at Xiamen Airport and Yunnan Zhaotong Airport.

With proven solutions in modern China, CNBM is prime to help Summit's South, East, and Central African markets reach toward a future of renewable energy.
