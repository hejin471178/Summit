About Summit

Summit Technology & Resources Inc. is a trading company focused on the acquisition of raw materials for super alloys.

and export of rare and strategic minerals with a focus on tantalum, tin, and tungsten. These minerals are sourced from East African regions—primarily Rwanda, Zambia, Nigeria, and Ethiopia—and processed in China before being delivered to industrial clients in the United States and Europe.

Summit acts as a commercial bridge between African producers, Chinese processors, and International buyers. Summit endeavors for secure, compliant, and efficient mineral supply chains. The company serves producers that rely on high-performance materials for sectors such as aerospace, semiconductors, renewable energy, and industrial gas turbines.

Our History

Summit Technology & Resources Inc's founder, Mr. Bing Lee, has been in the metal trading business since 2007 with the establishing of Platotex Technology Company Ltd. in Hong Kong. Since inception, Mr. Lee has traveled throughout the European Union and Africa. Accordia Ltd. was setup in the process to facilitate the trading of material international.

With the establishing of Summit on 7th of February 2025, Summit pushes forward the company's goals in renewable energy and high performance metal procurement, and the funding of materials for the super alloy industry. Summit is but one sister within a group of four as we endeavor to improve support for the global supply chain's need of rare metals for ultra-performance and high-technology applications.
