COMPANY PROFILE

Summit Technology & Resources Inc. is an operational mining and trading company focused on the acquisition and export of raw and strategic minerals with a focus on tantalum, tin, and tungsten. These minerals are sourced from East African regions—primarily Rwanda, Zambia, Nigeria, and Ethiopia—and processed in China before being delivered to industrial clients in the United States and Europe.

Summit acts as a commercial bridge between African producers, Chinese processors, and International buyers, enabling secure, compliant, and efficient mineral supply chains. The company serves end markets that rely on high-performance materials for sectors such as aerospace, semiconductors, renewable energy, and industrial gas turbines.
