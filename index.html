COMPANY PROFILE

Summit Technology & Resources Inc. is a specialist in strategic minerals. We act as a commercial bridge between African producers, Chinese processors, international buyers and end-users, enabling secure, compliant, and efficient mineral and metal on-demand supply chains. Our end markets rely on our supply of high-performance on-demand materials for industrial manufacturing sectors that include aerospace, semiconductors, renewable energy and gas turbines.

Our business roots began in 2007 at the heart of Hong Kong. Since then, we have grown in experience sourcing and establishing in China on materials such as post-transition metals and metals for high entropy alloys.

Our proud niche is in sourcing and delivering high-quality 3T (tantalum, tin, and tungsten) critical materials that power modern industries. Since 2013, we have established supply chains from Nigeria, Rwanda, Uganda, Burundi, Ethiopia and parts of the Democratic Republic of Congo. In addition, we have logistical connections between Kenya, Tanzania, China, Japan, Europe and the US.

Through strong partnerships across East Africa and advanced processing in China, we ensure a seamless supply chain that meets the highest standards of reliability and ethics. Our commitment to excellence enables leading manufacturers in the United States of America and the European Union to innovate with confidence.
